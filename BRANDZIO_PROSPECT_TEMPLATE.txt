SCORING AFTER THEY RESPOND:

Once you have all 8 answers, calculate prospect tier:

HOT (85–100):
- Budget: £15k+/month
- Industry: Dental or cosmetic clinics
- Pain point: Specific, solvable (not "everything")
- Conversion rate: Already tracking metrics (shows data maturity)
→ ACTION: Send audit + book call immediately

WARM (70–84):
- Budget: £5k–15k/month
- Industry: Luxury skin/hair, startups, e-commerce
- Pain point: Clear but not urgent
- Inquiry volume: 20+ per month
→ ACTION: Send audit + 2-week follow-up email

MAYBE (55–69):
- Budget: £3k–5k/month OR budget unknown
- Industry: Service-based but niche
- Pain point: Vague ("everything is hard")
- No clear conversion baseline
→ ACTION: Send audit, low priority follow-up

COLD (<55):
- Budget: <£3k/month
- Single-location service business
- No growth ambition signaled
- Not interested in AI/automation
→ ACTION: Archive or nurture list (6-month follow-up)
