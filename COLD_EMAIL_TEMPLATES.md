# HOW TO PERSONALIZE THESE

## High-Impact Changes

**Change 1: Reference their specific pain point**

Generic: "...struggling with [PAIN POINT]"

Personalized: "...I noticed most of your Instagram posts get ~X likes, but your conversion to consultations is only Y%. The issue is usually..." (shows you researched)

**Change 2: Use their actual numbers**

Generic: "...£X in new revenue"

Personalized: "...if we add 30% more inquiries to your current 18/month, that's ~27/month. At your 55% conversion rate, that's 15 new clients. At £280/month each, that's £4,200 in new monthly revenue."

**Change 3: Reference their specific business**

Generic: "...boutique clinics like yours"

Personalized: "...I see you specialize in cosmetic procedures. Most clinics like yours struggle with [SPECIFIC PAIN] because..."

**Change 4: Use specific product recommendations (Jaggo)**

Generic: "...mixed Kashmiri crewel..."

Personalized: "...based on your Berlin store's aesthetic, I'd recommend 60 Kashmiri crewel bags, 50 heritage textiles, and 40 pieces of contemporary art décor. That would cost roughly £6,500 wholesale."

---

# BEST PRACTICES

✅ **Keep it short** - 3-5 short paragraphs max
✅ **Use their language** - Match their tone (luxury/casual/professional)
✅ **Show you researched** - Reference their Instagram, website, or product
✅ **Make CTA clear** - "15-min call?" or "Worth a chat?"
✅ **Don't oversell** - Let them come to you
✅ **Add social proof (optional)** - "We work with [Similar Brand]" (if true)
✅ **Personalize subject line** - Use their company name, not generic
