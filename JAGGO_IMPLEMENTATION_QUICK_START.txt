QUICK START: The 5-MINUTE JAGGO SETUP

================================================================================

STEP 1: GET THE JAGGO SKILL
You already have: JAGGO_BUYER_MATCHER_SKILL.md
This is your "playbook" for scoring buyers.

STEP 2: CREATE JAGGO BUYER INTAKE FORM
Based on the 10 questions in JAGGO_BUYER_MATCHER_SKILL.md, create a form:

Questions to collect from each buyer:

1. Company Name
2. Buyer Type (Luxury retailer / Hospitality / Importer / Reseller / Other)
3. Primary Market (Germany / UK / Scandinavia / France / Other)
4. Store Focus (Home décor / Ethical lifestyle / Yoga/wellness / Museum gift / etc)
5. Est. Annual Order Value (£0-5k / £5-15k / £15-50k / £50k+)
6. Lead Time Flexibility (Flexible / 30-45 days / Rush only)
7. Sustainability Priority (Critical / Important / Nice-to-have)
8. Website/Instagram Handle
9. Decision Maker Name & Email
10. Current Sourcing Challenge

Deploy this as:
- Manual form for your internal use
- Or Typeform for prospects to self-fill

STEP 3: USE THE MATCHING ALGORITHM
For each buyer:
1. Fill in the 10 fields
2. Use the scoring formula in JAGGO_BUYER_MATCHER_SKILL.md
3. Calculate their score (0-100)
4. Assign tier: 🔥 HOT (85+) | 🟢 WARM (70-84) | 🟡 MAYBE (55-69) | 🔵 COLD (<55)

STEP 4: GENERATE MATCH CARD
For each buyer (especially HOT tier):
1. Use the "OUTPUT SCHEMA" from JAGGO_BUYER_MATCHER_SKILL.md
2. Generate match card with:
   - Score + Tier
   - Product recommendation
   - Buyer persona & pain points
   - Personalized email template

STEP 5: SEND + LOG
1. Send personalized email to HOT tier buyers
2. Log in Google Sheet: Jaggo Buyers tab
3. Track replies & book calls with Vinod
