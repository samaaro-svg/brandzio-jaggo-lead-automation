## BASE RETAINER PRICING (Monthly)

### TIER 1: DENTAL CLINICS (Premium Positioning)
Monthly Retainer: £3,500 - £4,500
Minimum Engagement: 6 months
Total Cost (6 months): £21,000 - £27,000

- High inquiry volume (15-25/month baseline)

- Clear ROI: +30% inquiries = +£2,250-3,600 monthly revenue
- Highly structured sales process (consultations → conversions)

What's included:
✓ 2x monthly strategy calls
✓ WhatsApp/SMS automation setup
✓ Email nurture sequences
✓ Instagram Reels content planning (2-3/week)
✓ Conversion rate optimization
✓ Monthly reporting dashboard

## SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES & DISCOUNTS

### UPFRONT PAYMENT DISCOUNT
If prospect pays 6 months upfront:
- Deduct 10% from total contract value

Example:
- Standard 6-month (Dental): £21,000 - £27,000
- Upfront 10% discount: £18,900 - £24,300

### PILOT ENGAGEMENT (3-Month Option)
For prospects hesitant about 6-month commitment:
- Offer 3-month pilot at 15% premium to standard rate

Example:
- Standard monthly (Dental): £3,500
- 3-month pilot: (£3,500 × 1.15) × 3 = £12,075
- Then: Upgrade to 6-month at standard rate if successful

### TEAM SCALING ADD-ON
If prospect has multiple locations or departments:
- Base retainer: £[AMOUNT]
- Per additional location/department: +£500-800/month

Example:
- Single dental clinic: £3,500/month
- 3 dental clinics: (£3,500 × 3) + £300 coordination = £10,800/month

### PRODUCT/SERVICE LAUNCH ADD-ON
If prospect is launching new service during engagement:
- Add £1,000-2,000 one-time launch project
- Includes: Content strategy, go-to-market plan, email sequence

## PRICING BY PROSPECT TIER (Quick Assignment)

### HOT PROSPECT (Score 85-100)
Fit: Dental/Cosmetic clinic, £15k+/month marketing budget, clear metrics
→ Recommend: **TIER 1 or 2 pricing** (£3,200-£4,500/month)
→ Pitch: 6-month engagement, performance bonus available
→ Close rate: 60-70% of discussions

### WARM PROSPECT (Score 70-84)
Fit: Luxury skin/hair, startups, £5k-15k/month budget, some metrics
→ Recommend: **TIER 3 or 4 pricing** (£2,500-£3,800/month)
→ Pitch: 6-month engagement with 3-month pilot option
→ Close rate: 40-50% of discussions

### MAYBE PROSPECT (Score 55-69)
Fit: Smaller budget, unclear metrics, niche vertical
→ Recommend: **TIER 4 pricing + pilot** (£2,500/month × 3)
→ Pitch: "Let's test with a 3-month pilot first"
→ Close rate: 20-30% of discussions

### COLD PROSPECT (Score <55)
Fit: Budget <£3k, no growth ambition, wrong vertical
→ ACTION: Nurture list (6-month follow-up)
→ Don't pitch yet; send monthly value content
→ Revisit when they show readiness

## PRICING NEGOTIATION RULES

✅ OKAY TO NEGOTIATE:
- Lock in lower monthly rate if they commit 12 months (instead of 6)
- Bundle services (e.g., ads + content + automation at discount)
- Offer performance bonus (if we hit X inquiries, they pay Y extra)
- Discount if they're a referral or case study

❌ DO NOT NEGOTIATE (Stand Firm):
- Don't drop below:
  - Dental/Cosmetic: £2,800/month
  - Luxury skin/hair: £2,200/month
  - Startups: £2,000/month
- Don't reduce below 6-month minimum engagement
- Don't bundle "free" services beyond what's promised
- Don't compete on price with other agencies (compete on results)

## CONVERSATION EXAMPLES

### How to Present Pricing (Discovery Call)

**OPENER:**
"Based on your current state, here's what we typically charge for your type of business:

For dental clinics like yours, a 6-month engagement is £3,500-4,500/month.
Based on our chat, I'd recommend the lower end: £3,500/month.

That's because we're focused on 3 quick wins in the first 90 days.
Once those are working, most clients see an extra 30-50% in inquiries within 3 months.

For you, that's roughly £2,250-3,600 in new monthly revenue.
So your investment pays for itself in less than a month.

Does that make sense to you?"

**IF THEY SAY "Too expensive":**
"I hear you. Let's talk about what the cost of NOT doing this is.

You're getting [CURRENT_INQUIRIES] inquiries/month and converting [CURRENT_CONVERSION]%.
That's [CURRENT_REVENUE] in monthly revenue from new clients.

If we improve that by even 20% (very conservative), that's [IMPROVED_REVENUE] extra monthly.
Over 6 months, that's £[TOTAL_VALUE].

So the real question isn't whether you can afford £3,500/month—
it's whether you can afford NOT to.

How does that land?"

**IF THEY SAY "Can we do 3 months?":**
"I get it. Most first-time clients want to test the waters.

Here's what I suggest:
Let's do a 3-month pilot at £4,025/month (15% premium for the shorter commitment).
That's £12,075 total.

If we hit the targets we outlined, you upgrade to 6-month at the standard £3,500 rate.
If we don't hit targets, we part ways—no hard feelings.

Fair?"

**IF THEY SAY "I need to think about it":**
"Totally fair. Here's what I'd suggest:

Let's lock in this pricing for 14 days. After that, prices may adjust.
Give yourself a few days to think, then we can circle back Wednesday.

Does that work?"

"If we hit 50+ new inquiries/month by Month 6, you bonus us £2,500.
